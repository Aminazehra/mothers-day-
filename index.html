Dear Mom,

From my first breath, you were my world. You held my hand when I was scared, whispered prayers when I was sick, and gave me the strength to chase dreams.

I still remember how you stayed awake during my exams, quietly brought me kehwa, and reminded me that I could do anything. You made sacrifices I can never repay — only appreciate.

Even now, your wisdom guides me. Your strength lives in me. Thank you for being my light, my comfort, and my forever home.

With all my heart,
Amina
